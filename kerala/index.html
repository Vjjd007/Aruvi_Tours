+

+

Morning drive to Alleppey (1.5 hrs). Board your private, premium houseboat by noon. Glide through network of canals watching rural Kerala life unfold. Freshly cooked Kerala lunch onboard. Sunset cruise into open lake. Overnight stay on the houseboat under a canopy of stars.
