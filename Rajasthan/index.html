Palaces, Desert & Culture
Royal Rajasthan

Experience the grandeur of Rajasthan — a tapestry of magnificent forts, shimmering deserts, and timeless royal traditions. This curated 6N/7D package brings the region's rich heritage to life in comfort and style.

From sunrise at Amber Fort to a twilight camel safari in the Thar, from lavish palace stays to intimate folk performances — each day is crafted to reveal Rajasthan's royal spirit. Handpicked heritage hotels, authentic Rajasthani cuisine, and private guided tours await.
