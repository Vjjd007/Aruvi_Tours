More than a travel agency —
a promise to the world.

1000+ Happy Travellers
50+ Destinations
8+ Years of Trust

Aruvi Tours is a Salem-based premium travel company dedicated to the art of purposeful exploration. Our name — drawn from the Tamil word for waterfall — reflects our belief that travel, like water, should flow naturally, beautifully, and without boundaries.

We curate experiences across India's most iconic landscapes and the world's most coveted destinations — from the serene backwaters of Kerala to the ancient souks of Morocco, and the sun-kissed coastlines of Europe. Every journey we design carries the hallmark of refined taste and attentive care.

We don't simply sell trips. We listen, understand, and craft each itinerary around the traveller — their pace, their passions, their stories waiting to be written.

2016 Est. Salem, Tamil Nadu

Our Story

Born from a passion for
discovery and wonder.

Aruvi Tours began with a single conviction — that travel should be accessible without sacrificing elegance. Founded in Salem with a small team and an immense heart, we started by offering curated group tours to South India's most beloved spiritual and natural destinations.

Word spread quietly at first — through the gratitude of families who returned home with stories, and honeymooners who found their perfect escape. Each journey refined our craft. Each traveller taught us something new about the world and about hospitality.

Today, Aruvi Tours stands as a trusted name across Tamil Nadu and beyond — GST registered, MSME certified, and proudly affiliated with the Salem Tour Operators Association. Our growth has always been measured not in numbers, but in the smiles we've carried home.

"We didn't set out to build a company. We set out to change how people experience the world."

— Founder, Aruvi Tours
